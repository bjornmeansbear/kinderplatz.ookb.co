Kinderplatz Supports Federal and Military Families

Kinderplatz partners with GSA and the National Association of Child Care Resource & Referral Agencies (NACCRRA) to assist Federal and Active Duty Military families with subsidy funding for weekly tuition fees. The GSA Child Care Subsidy provides support for the following federal agencies:

  • GSA for GSA associates

  • The US Coast Guard for Active Duty Members

  • The US Army for Active Duty personnel and Army DOD civilians

  • The US Army Reserve and Army National Guard for activated members

  • NACCRRA for Active Duty Air Force, Marines, and Navy

To learn about eligibility requirements, please contact Kinderplatz via phone or e-mail.

Kinderplatz recognizes that Active Duty Military families face unique challenges, especially with regard to finding and affording high quality child care. To further support our Military families, long-term teaching staff at Kinderplatz has received specialized deployment training through SURGE. This training has helped teachers learn how to best support children and their families as they cope with deployment — before, during and after, military moves, and other unique circumstances related to the active duty military lifestyle.
